Why Is Hackchella A Must-Attend?

If you're a college student or recent graduate who wants to contribute to a greater cause, make a difference, add value or have an impact, you're invited to Hackchella. It's a 48-hour immersive hackathon where technologists, designers and entrepreneurs collaborate to solve real-world problems using a variety of software and hardware tools.

Hack

Hackers will learn and build on a variety of emerging APIs, software and hardware tools.

Create

Hackers will utilize various technology stacks and frameworks to solve real-world problems.

Play

Hackers will have an unforgettable experience while innovating across all industry sectors.
